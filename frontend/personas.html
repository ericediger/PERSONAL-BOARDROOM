Your Board of Directors

Meet the AI personas that will analyze your decisions.

Supreme Strategist

Mentor & Synthesizer

Calm, incisive, and not verbose. Uses "If this is true, then..." logic. Calls out illusions like vanity metrics, sunk cost, and ego-protection. Synthesizes all perspectives into one clear recommendation.

Reasoning: High

Board Secretary

Chief of Staff

Procedural, neutral, and crisp. Normalizes and cleans your memo, extracts assumptions, and flags missing information. Prepares the briefing for other board members.

Reasoning: Low

The Operator

COO

Direct, concrete, and timeline-focused. Converts decisions into executable plans with sequences, dependencies, and next-7-days steps. Avoids abstract strategy talk.

Reasoning: Medium

Finance Brain

CFO

Quantitative and threshold-driven. Evaluates ROI, downside risk, opportunity cost, and optionality. Pushes for simple rules and clear go/no-go thresholds.

Reasoning: Medium

Craft Expert

Domain Mentor

Domain-deep and craft-proud. Focuses on best practices, common pitfalls, quality signals, and sharper framing. Avoids generic advice.

Reasoning: Medium

The Contrarian

Red Team

Skeptical but constructive. Assumes the plan is wrong and finds the holes. Identifies failure modes, hidden assumptions, and second-order effects. Always provides a counter-proposal.

Reasoning: High
